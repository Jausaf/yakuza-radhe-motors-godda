# YAKUZA Electric Scooters - Radhe Motors Website Design Strategy

## Design Philosophy: "Electric Velocity"

**Theme:** Premium EV Dealership with Modern, High-Energy Aesthetic

### Color Palette
- **Primary Green:** Vibrant electric lime green (#00FF41) - Energy, eco-friendly, modern
- **Secondary Black:** Deep charcoal (#1a1a1a) - Premium, professional, contrast
- **Accent White:** Clean white (#FFFFFF) - Clarity, trust, tech-forward
- **Highlight Cyan:** Electric cyan (#00D9FF) - Technology, innovation, futuristic

### Typography
- **Headings:** Poppins Bold (modern, geometric, tech-forward)
- **Body:** Inter Regular (clean, readable, professional)
- **Accent:** Space Mono (monospace for tech specs and pricing)

### Design Elements
- **Logo:** YAKUZA stylized Y with electric bolt
- **Layout:** Asymmetric, dynamic angles, motion-inspired
- **Animations:** Smooth, energetic transitions reflecting EV speed
- **Dividers:** Diagonal cuts, lightning bolt patterns
- **Icons:** Minimalist, tech-focused EV-specific icons

### Brand Voice
- **Tagline:** "Ride the Future, Live the Freedom"
- **Tone:** Modern, energetic, trustworthy, innovative
- **Examples:**
  - "Experience the Future of Urban Mobility"
  - "Eco-Smart. Performance-Driven. Affordable."

### Signature Brand Color
**Electric Lime Green (#00FF41)** - Unmistakably YAKUZA's premium EV identity

### Layout Paradigm
- Hero section with dynamic scooter showcase
- Asymmetric card layouts for models
- Diagonal section transitions
- Floating elements with parallax effects
- Energy-forward spacing and rhythm

### Interaction Philosophy
- Smooth, snappy button responses
- Hover effects that feel responsive and premium
- Micro-interactions that celebrate the EV experience
- Quick-loading, high-performance feel

### Animation Guidelines
- Button hover: Scale 1.05 with green glow
- Section transitions: Smooth fade-in with slide-up
- Model cards: Lift effect on hover
- Pricing tables: Highlight animation on selection
- CTA buttons: Pulse effect to draw attention
